The city my grandparents met in, versus the city I grew up in


Laguna Beach, known as an Artist's Colony, was founded on June 29, 1927. Both a Mormon settlement and place for drug experiments, the mixed culture and scenic coastal setting lead it to become a tourist destination. Laguna Beach gained further popularity in the early 2000s with the popular Laguna Beach Show by MTV. Pictured above is the downtown area on Pacific Coast Highway, which includes structures still present today. Some include the White House restaurant and Hotel Laguna.
